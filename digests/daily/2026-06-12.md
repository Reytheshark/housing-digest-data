## Key Baseline Facts
- **San Diego city:** median sale $954K 3-mo (-3.0% YoY); SDAR attached median $680K (+1.5%) but attached DOM +24% YoY — the attached segment is where softness lives.
- **UTC:** Q1-26 condo median sale $630K, **-11.9% YoY** (n=42). 50 condos active $325K–$1.65M. The single best fit for the $400–750K band in San Diego, and currently buyer-leaning.
- **Carmel Valley:** scope band is very thin — most attached stock sits $950K–$1.3M. Low-HOA product exists (e.g., Cambria ~$275/mo) but rarely under $750K.
- **Loudoun:** May closed 537 (+6.8%), median $790K (+2.0%), DOM 13, actives -8.8%, 1.8 months supply — firmly seller-favored.
- **Arcola/Stone Ridge:** TH medians $676–682K squarely in band; micro DOM (63) runs much longer than county (13), suggesting less frenzy in Dulles South than headline Loudoun. Van Metre opens a new Stone Ridge phase Fall 2026 — future supply.
- **Fairfax:** county median $813K (+3.4%), DOM 25; NVAR/GMU forecast **condo prices -2.7% in 2026 on rising HOA fees** — directly relevant to the low-HOA thesis.
- **Oakton HS area:** Oakton median sale $793K (Mar, n=34), Zillow typical $1.10M, pendings in ~5 days; in-band attached stock is scarce and concentrated near Vienna Metro. School zoning must be verified per listing.
- **Dallas:** attached is the soft spot — DFW townhome median -4.3% YoY, condo -9.8%; condo months supply 7.7. Citywide medians ($465K, +5.6%) mask it.
- **Austin:** deepest buyer market in scope — 5.9 months supply overall, 51.95% of actives with a price cut, condos at $390K median (-6.5%), 7.48 months, 108 DOM, selling at 95.7% of list.

## Micro vs. Metro Divergence
UTC trades at a deep discount to its parent ($689 vs $687 sale ppsf looks par, but condo prices are down 11.9% YoY vs city -3.0%) — the micro is correcting faster than the metro. Arcola inverts Loudoun's picture: in-band townhomes sit 63 days vs the county's 13, so band-specific leverage exists even inside a seller's county. Carmel Valley and Oakton both out-tighten their parents but offer almost nothing in band — they're watch-only markets until inventory appears.
