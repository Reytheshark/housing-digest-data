*No daily runs occurred 6/16–6/21; this weekly is built from fresh 6/22 research vs. the 6/15 baseline. WoW deltas are 7-day comparisons. Daily digest also at digests/daily/2026-06-22.md.*

## Executive Summary

Mortgage rates dipped to **6.47%** (Freddie Mac PMMS June 19), down 34 bps YoY, but the Fed's June 17 FOMC under new Chair Warsh delivered a hawkish pivot: the 2026 rate cut was removed from the dot plot and a possible rate hike was signaled (median dot 3.8% year-end) -- the most significant macro shift of the week. UTC (San Diego 92122) remains the highest-leverage buyer micro-market in scope: Q1-2026 condos -11.9% YoY vs. the city's -4.4%, nearly 3x faster. Arcola Town Center (20166) holds as the only NoVA micro-market with active in-scope inventory (12 TH, HOA ~$351/mo, median ~$585K). Oakton HS watches remain pending on HOA and FCPS boundary. Dallas and Austin are the deepest buyer markets in scope (condo -9.8% and -6.5% YoY; DOM 70 and 108). Most notable divergence: Arcola vs. Loudoun Co. median ($585K vs. $790K, 26% discount; DOM 49 vs. 13) -- the widest buyer-advantage gap in the NoVA scope.

## Listing Flow & Absorption

**San Diego city:** 1,948 active TH+condo all prices; in-scope subset ($400K-$750K, HOA <=400/mo) est. ~300-400 units. HOA exclusion rate significant: SB 326 + insurance costs push many condos above the $550/mo cap.

**Carmel Valley:** Zero in-scope inventory. Price floors (Cambria $950K+, Camino Villas $700K+) make $400K-$750K structurally empty. No flow or absorption to track.

**UTC (92122):** ~86 active; 42 condo closings in Q1-2026 (~14/month). At 86 active / 14 per month = approx. 6 months supply -- buyer-favorable. The $400K-$750K band captures a good share of the 75-condo pool ($325K-$1.65M range).

**Arcola (20166):** 12 TH active; DOM 49; 2 offers typical. At ~1-2 closings/month implied, supply approx. 6-12 months -- moderate, balanced.

**Oakton HS area:** Extremely limited in-scope TH/condo inventory. 3 watchlist items ($455K-$740K) with no movement. HOA unknowns prevent clean absorption calculation.

**Dallas (city):** ~1,508 condos+TH; 70-day DOM; 25% with price cuts. Liquid buyer market. $400K-$750K captures most city scope (median $444K).

**Austin (city):** ~1,337 condos+TH; 69-day city DOM; 108-day condo DOM; 7.48 months condo supply; 52% price cuts. Deepest buyer market in scope.

## Implications & Positioning

**For a buyer:**
- Best leverage now: UTC (92122) -- -11.9% YoY; ~6 months supply; 6.7% gross rental yield cushion.
- Best NoVA entry: Arcola (20166) -- $524K-$585K; HOA $351/mo confirmed; 49-day DOM allows negotiation.
- Best cash-flow potential: Dallas condos -- -9.8% YoY; 70-day DOM; builder incentives available.
- Most patient play: Austin condos -- DOM 108; 7.48 months supply; significant negotiating room.
- Rate risk: Fed dot plot puts 25bp hike in play; would add ~$80-100/mo to P&I on $460K loan.

**For a seller/investor:**
- Loudoun TH sellers: hold pricing; DOM 13 and +2.5% YoY TH forecast support it.
- Dallas/Austin investors: caution -- builder incentives from new construction will pressure resale prices through at least year-end 2026 (TRERC/Builder Magazine).
